Welcome to the BBIS Community

From your professors and mentors to your friends and teammates, BBIS at Kathmandu University is a vibrant community where you can feel supported and encouraged. Located at the scenic Dhulikhel campus under DOMIC, BBIS offers a unique blend of management education and technological innovation.

No matter where you come from, BBIS is home. Our diverse student body creates a collaborative environment where future business leaders and technology innovators learn, grow, and thrive together.

Through clubs, events, projects, and everyday interactions, you'll build lasting friendships, develop professional skills, and create memories that last a lifetime. This is more than just education—it's a transformative experience.

Careers through BBIS

Over 70% of BBIS students actively participate in innovation-driven activities, from competing in the Hult Prize to launching startups through the entrepreneurship club. Our students don't just learn about business and technology—they create it.

Whether it's building robots, developing apps, participating in case competitions, or pitching business ideas, BBIS students are makers, thinkers, and doers who are shaping the future of Nepal's business and tech landscape.
